VesselPDA'nın profesyonel landing page'ini yeniden tasarla ve SEO optimizasyonu yap.

Yapılacaklar:

1. client/src/pages/landing.tsx dosyasını tamamen yeniden yaz:

   HERO SECTION:
   - Büyük başlık: "The Maritime Platform Built for Modern Shipping"
   - Alt başlık: "Manage vessels, port calls, disbursements, and crew — all in one place."
   - CTA butonları: "Start Free" (register'a git) + "Watch Demo" (demo mode'a git)
   - Arka plan: Koyu denizcilik teması, subtle animasyonlu dalga efekti (CSS)
   - Hero image: Dashboard screenshot veya harita mockup

   FEATURES SECTION (özellik kartları):
   - Vessel Management: "Track your fleet in real-time with AIS integration"
   - Proforma DA: "Calculate disbursement accounts instantly for any Turkish port"
   - Voyage Management: "Multi-port voyages with SOF, documents, and expense tracking"
   - Team Collaboration: "Invite agents, brokers, and crew to work together on voyages"
   - Compliance: "Stay compliant with ISM, ISPS, and MLC requirements"
   - Market Intelligence: "Compare port costs and track bunker prices"
   Her kart bir icon + başlık + kısa açıklama + "Learn More" link

   STATS SECTION:
   - API'den çek: Toplam kullanıcı, toplam gemi, toplam sefer, toplam liman
   - Animated counter (sayılar yukarı sayar)

   HOW IT WORKS (nasıl çalışır):
   - Step 1: Register & set up your company
   - Step 2: Add your vessels and fleet
   - Step 3: Create voyages and manage port calls
   - Step 4: Collaborate with agents and service providers
   Her step bir numara + başlık + kısa açıklama

   PRICING SECTION:
   - Free: 1 user, 3 vessels, 5 proformas/month
   - Standard: 5 users, unlimited vessels, unlimited proformas
   - Enterprise: Unlimited users, custom features, priority support
   Her plan bir kart, CTA butonu

   TESTIMONIALS (ileride doldurulacak):
   - 3 testimonial placeholder kartı
   - "Join hundreds of maritime professionals"

   CTA SECTION:
   - "Ready to streamline your maritime operations?"
   - "Start Free — No credit card required"
   - Register butonu

   FOOTER:
   - Logo + kısa açıklama
   - Links: Features, Pricing, Contact, Forum, Directory
   - Legal: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service
   - Social media ikonları (placeholder)
   - "© 2024 VesselPDA. All rights reserved."

2. SEO optimizasyonu:

   client/index.html güncelle:
   - <title>VesselPDA - Maritime Port Agency & Vessel Management Platform</title>
   - <meta name="description" content="Manage vessels, port calls, disbursement accounts, crew, and compliance. The all-in-one maritime platform for ship agents, brokers, and shipowners.">
   - <meta name="keywords" content="maritime, port agency, vessel management, disbursement account, proforma DA, ship agent, port call, AIS tracking, voyage management">
   - Open Graph meta tags (Facebook/LinkedIn paylaşım):
     og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url, og:type
   - Twitter Card meta tags
   - <link rel="canonical" href="https://vesselpda.com">

   client/src/components/page-meta.tsx güncelle:
   - Her sayfaya özel title ve description set etsin
   - Dashboard: "Dashboard | VesselPDA"
   - Vessels: "My Vessels | VesselPDA"
   - Proformas: "Proforma DA | VesselPDA"

3. Sitemap ve robots.txt:
   - server/routes'a GET /sitemap.xml endpoint'i ekle (public sayfaları listele)
   - client/public/robots.txt oluştur:
     User-agent: *
     Allow: /
     Sitemap: https://vesselpda.com/sitemap.xml
     Disallow: /api/

4. Performance:
   - Landing page'de lazy loading kullan (görseller ve alt bölümler)
   - Above-the-fold content hızlı yüklensin
   - Intersection Observer ile scroll animasyonları

5. Responsive tasarım:
   - Mobil: tek kolon, küçültülmüş hero
   - Tablet: 2 kolon grid
   - Desktop: tam genişlik, 3 kolon feature kartları

Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion kullan. shadcn/ui componentleri. Koyu denizcilik teması: #0B1120 arka plan, #38BDF8 accent, beyaz text.
