Her sefer icin detayli maliyet takibi sistemi ekle. Seferde olusan tum masraflarin kaydedilmesi ve butce ile karsilastirilmasi.

Yapilacaklar:

1. shared/schema.ts dosyasina ekle:

voyageExpenses tablosu:
- id (serial PK)
- voyageId (integer, FK voyages, not null)
- portCallId (integer, FK voyagePortCalls, nullable) — hangi limanda olustuysa
- userId (varchar, FK users, not null)
- organizationId (integer, FK organizations, nullable)
- category (text, not null) — "port_charges", "agency_fee", "pilotage", "tugboat", "mooring", "bunker", "provisions", "crew", "repairs", "insurance", "communication", "misc"
- description (text, not null)
- budgetAmount (real, nullable) — planlanan tutar
- actualAmount (real, not null) — gercek tutar
- currency (text, default "USD")
- exchangeRate (real, default 1)
- amountUsd (real) — USD karsiligi (otomatik hesapla)
- vendor (text, nullable) — tedarikci/firma
- invoiceNumber (text, nullable)
- invoiceDate (timestamp, nullable)
- receiptFileUrl (text, nullable) — makbuz/fatura dosyasi
- paymentStatus (text, default "unpaid") — "unpaid", "paid", "partially_paid"
- paidAt (timestamp, nullable)
- notes (text, nullable)
- createdAt (timestamp, default now)

voyageBudgets tablosu:
- id (serial PK)
- voyageId (integer, FK voyages, not null)
- category (text, not null)
- budgetAmount (real, not null)
- currency (text, default "USD")
- notes (text)
- createdAt (timestamp, default now)

2. Endpoint'ler:

GET /api/voyages/:id/expenses — Sefer masraflari listesi (kategoriye gore gruplu)
POST /api/voyages/:id/expenses — Yeni masraf ekle
PATCH /api/voyages/:id/expenses/:expenseId — Masraf guncelle
DELETE /api/voyages/:id/expenses/:expenseId — Masraf sil
GET /api/voyages/:id/expenses/summary — Kategori bazli masraf ozeti + butce karsilastirma

GET /api/voyages/:id/budgets — Sefer butcesi
POST /api/voyages/:id/budgets — Butce kalemi ekle/guncelle (upsert by category)
GET /api/voyages/:id/expenses/pdf — Masraf raporu PDF

3. client/src/pages/voyage-detail.tsx'e "Masraflar" tab'i ekle:

   Ozet Kartlari:
   - Toplam Butce: $XX,XXX
   - Toplam Harcama: $XX,XXX
   - Kalan Butce: $X,XXX (yesil) veya Butce Asimi: $X,XXX (kirmizi)
   - Odenmis / Odenmemis

   Kategori Bazli Tablo:
   | Kategori | Butce | Harcama | Fark | Durum |
   | Liman Masraflari | $5,000 | $4,800 | -$200 | ✅ |
   | Acente Ucreti | $2,000 | $2,500 | +$500 | ⚠️ |
   Her kategori tiklaninca alt kalemlere acilir (accordion)

   Butce vs Gercek Bar Chart (recharts): Yan yana cubuk grafik

   Masraf Ekleme Dialog:
   - Kategori secimi
   - Aciklama, tutar, para birimi
   - Tedarikci, fatura no
   - Makbuz/fatura dosyasi yukleme
   - Odeme durumu

   Liman Bazli Gruplama: Multi-port seferlerde her limanin masraflari ayri gosterilsin

4. Masraf eklendiginde otomatik amountUsd hesapla (exchange rate kullanarak).

5. Masraf raporu PDF: Gemi bilgileri, liman bazli masraf tablosu, kategori bazli ozet, toplam, butce karsilastirma.
